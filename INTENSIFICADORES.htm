INTENSIFIERS


WHAT ARE INTENSIFIERS?

In grammar, it is applied to a word, especially an adjective or adverb, which has little semantic content of its own, but which serves to intensify the meaning of the word or phrase that is modified: terribly and even intensifying sentences that are terribly sad and disorderly above.

INTENSIFIERS

  • VERY = MUY
  • SO = TAN
  • TOO = DEMACIADO
  • QUITE = BASTANTE
  • PRETTY = BASTANTE
  • FAIRLY = BASTANTE
  • RATHER = ALGO/POCO
  • ALITTL = UN POCO
  • ENOUGH = SUFICIENTE
